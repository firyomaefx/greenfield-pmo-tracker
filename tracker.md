## Other Notable New Entrants (2025–2026)

### Kulim (KHTP)
- **Ferrotec – Plant #2**: Apr 2025 groundbreaking. RM1B investment, 1M sq ft on 20 acres, 700+ CNC machines, Industry 4.0; >1,000 high-value jobs. Completion ~2026.
- **Ichia Technologies – 2nd Facility**: Oct 2025 grand opening. RM490M, 55,000+ sq m on 11 acres; PCBs for automotive & telecom; ~600 jobs. AI & IoT enabled.
- **AT&S KHTP Campus**: May 2025 high-volume manufacturing start. RM5B total; IC substrates for AMD; ~1,500 employees; targeting top-3 global substrate provider.
- **Hyundai Motor**: RM2.16B first Malaysian assembly plant in Kulim; 7 CKD models within 5 years.
- **Unigen**: Oct 2024 groundbreaking; 56,000 sq ft facility to expand production capacity.
- **Pivotal Systems**: Late 2024/2025; new engineering & manufacturing facility for gas-flow solutions.

### Batu Kawan (BKIP / Valdor)
- **Chipbond Technology**: Feb 2026 official opening. ~USD200M (RM800M) advanced OSAT facility at Valdor Industrial Park. Capacity: 10,000 wafers + 100M WLCSP units/month.
- **Hotayi Electronic – Phase 3**: Mar 2025. RM500M expansion; 380,000 sq ft smart factory; 1,000 new high-skilled jobs; AI-driven analytics & IoT.
- **Benchmark Precision Technologies – PT4**: Feb 2025 groundbreaking at BKIP. USD25M (~RM110M), 215,000 sq ft; completion June 2026; 500+ jobs over 5 years. WFE manufacturing.

## 2026 Latest Updates

### Novolyte
- **Jan 19 2026**: Groundbreaking for new battery electrolyte plant at KHTP.
- Investment: **RM264 million**.
- Annual capacity: **~30,000 metric tons**.
- Targets domestic and Southeast Asian EV battery markets.
- Commissioning targeted for **Q3 2026**.

### AIXTRON
- **Mar 25 2026**: Announced plans to build a **new manufacturing facility in Malaysia (Penang region)**.
- Investment: **~EUR 40 million**.
- Capex to be spent in **H2 2026 and H1 2027**.
- Scope: local assembly & test of 100/150/200 mm compound semiconductor deposition equipment.
- Plant start-up: **Spring 2027**; first shipments: **H2 2027**.

### UMediC Group Berhad
- **Jan 2026**: Secured **3.06-acre** industrial site in Batu Kawan Industrial Park via 60-year lease for **RM11.4 million**.
- New plant to expand medical consumables & life-science business.
- Construction targeted **H2 2027**, completion **H1 2029**.
- **Aug 2025**: Unveiled expansion at existing Batu Kawan premises — doubled manufacturing capacity by adding 20,000 sq ft (total 50,000 sq ft). Added new cleanroom, UMC Healthcare Centre, Rescue Medic Ambulance & Learning Centre.
- **FY2025**: Revenue RM48.6m; net income RM8.13m.
- **1Q FY26**: PAT RM1.9m on revenue RM14.2m (+8.5% YoY).
